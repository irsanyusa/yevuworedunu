Shakira Sets Dates for Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran World Tour

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Shakira is ready to reunite with her “Shewolves” on tour.

After announcing the 2024 tour during her surprise appearance at Coachella with Bizarrap, Shakira shared the first slate of tour dates on Tuesday morning. Shakira will start the 14-date trek on Nov. 2 in Palm Desert, California (Acrisure Arena), and includes stops in Los Angeles (Kia Forum), Miami (Kaseya Center), Toronto (Scotiabank Arena), Brooklyn (Barclays Center), Chicago (United Center) and more.

The “Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran World Tour” will see Shakira performing songs from her new album and her catalog of hits. This tour, like Shakira’s newly announced “Las Mujeres Ya No Lloran” album, is her first since 2018’s “El Dorado” global outing. Shakira previously spoke with Billboard about the tour describing it as the “tour of my life.” Moreover, she also revealed that she was “taking notes” while watching Beyoncé’s “Renaissance” tour.

Despite the hiatus, Shakira has kept herself busy in the last two years. In 2023, Shakira won song of the year and best pop song for “Music Sessions Vol. 53” and best urban fusion/performance for “TQG” at the 2023 Latin Grammy Awards. “Music Sessions Vol. 53” also became the Spanish language song with the most streams in a single day in Spotify’s history.

Tickets will be available on Ticketmaster.com starting with a Citi presale on April 17 with additional presales running throughout the week ahead of the general sale beginning April 22.
