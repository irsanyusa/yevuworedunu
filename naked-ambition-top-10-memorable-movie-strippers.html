Naked Ambition: Top 10 Memorable Movie Strippers

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Director: Robert Rodriguez

Year Released: 1996

Studio: Dimension Films

Cast: George Clooney, Quentin Tarantino, Juliette Lewis

Get This Movie

Played by: Salma Hayek

Once you get past the rather fanciful conceit of George Clooney and Quentin Tarantino (who wrote the movie) being cast as brothers, From Dusk Till Dawn is a rather effective mid-budget horror-action flick: weird, violent and deliriously over the top. Clooney and Tarantino are bank robbers on the lam. After a blood-soaked crime spree, the boys escape into Mexico and await their contact at a strip club in a remote desert remote location. Once inside, they join a boisterous crowd on hand to witness the multitudinous talents of the club’s featured performer. Who, like all the other club employees, just happens to be a nasty vampire. For a generation of impressionable lads, Salma Hayek, playing the fantastically named Santánico Pandemónium, was the stuff of dreams—and nightmares.
