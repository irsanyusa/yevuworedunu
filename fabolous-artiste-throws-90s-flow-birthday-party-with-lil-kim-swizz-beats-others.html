Artiste Throws 90s Flow Birthday Party With Lil Kim, Swizz Beats, Others

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Fabolous held a star studded 90′s themed birthday party in New York City last night. To give that authentic 90′s feel, Kid Capri was spinning tunes all night and Ralph Mc Daniels along with the Video Music Box cameras were in the party doing interviews like they did back in the days. Guests included, Lil Kim, Swizz Beatz, Mase, Raekwon, Teyana Taylor, Ne-Yo, Lil Mama, DJ Khaled, Rico Love, Emily B, Remy Ma, Mack Wilds, Troy Ave, Ed Lover, DJ Clue, Lil Cease, AraabMuzik and more. Guests were in for an epic surprise when, Raekwon, Mase, Lil Kim, Remy Ma, Fabolous & Troy Ave gave impromptu performances. This was truly a night to remember and a big moment for Hip Hop.
