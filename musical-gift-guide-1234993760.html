The Best Broadway Merch to Gift Your Favorite Theater Kid This Year

Publish date: 2024-07-09

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Social Goods, the online store for activist clothes and accessories, has launched a special collection that will help send more New York City students to Broadway productions before they graduate high school.

For every purchase from the Arts Advocate capsule, designed in collaboration with Prinkshop, 25% of proceeds will be donated to the 2022 Broadway production of “Macbeth” and their Broadway Bridges’ initiative to provide students with theater tickets free of charge. The limited-edition collection features tees, long-sleeve shirts, totes and stickers — all overlayed with the Arts Advocate branding in pink lettering. Prices range in price between $4 for a sticker and $80 for a sweatshirt, all available on Social Goods’ website.

The collection comes as more people are headed back to the theater: dozens of highly anticipated shows have reopened in the past few months after shuttering their doors amidst the pandemic. In addition to “Macbeth,” which has been temporarily cancelled after Daniel Craig contracted COVID-19 this week, new shows include a Beanie Feldstein-helmed “Funny Girl” and an all-new “Wicked” starring Ariana Grande and Cynthia Erivo.

Check out the Social Goods x Macbeth capsule below, in addition to more Broadway swag to gift your favorite theater kid this year.
