Norwegian Joy Is One of the Worlds Greatest Places

Publish date: 2024-07-20

A go-kart track at sea? The idea might sound a bit wild, but that didn’t stop Norwegian Cruise Line from building one into the Norwegian Joy back in 2017. Now, after a $50 million 2019 renovation, the ship has even more to keep passengers entertained. The Joy is built to delight the most discerning of cruise guests—teenagers. Look for the Galaxy Pavilion virtual-reality and gaming center, as well as an open-air laser-tag arena and a retro American diner. —Alejandro de la Garza
