Who Is Jillian Michaels' Girlfriend? Meet Fashion Designer Deshanna Marie Minuto

Publish date: 2024-07-02

In the sweet selfie, Jillian's snuggled close to her girlfriend, designer Deshanna Marie Minuto. (BTW Jillian's rep confirmed their relationship earlier this summer to US Weekly .)

Deshanna has been cozying up with Jillian in photos on Instagram since late last year, but there's much more to know about the new love in the trainer's life.

Deshanna is a fashion designer.

She founded Letterino. The line features "reconstructed vintage jackets" and is based out of New York City according to the website . The custom jackets have hand embroidery and price tags hovering around $750.

Jillian and Deshanna have documented their travels with selfies.

The duo went public with their relationship with photos shared to Instagram in December 2018. Since then, they've visited Japan, Belize, and Spain together. She captioned one of many selfies: "I YOU @jillianmichaels". And Jillian commented: "you sure do... you spoil me"

The couple got together just a few months after Jillian went public with her split from fiance Heidi Rhoades . However, Jillian cleared up any rumors on Instagram , saying: "Heidi and I have been split for awhile now. Weve found were better friends and parents living apart than staying together.
