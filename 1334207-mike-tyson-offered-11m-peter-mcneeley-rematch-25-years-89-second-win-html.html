Mike Tyson offered $1.1m for Peter McNeeley rematch 25 years on from 89-second-win

Publish date: 2024-08-08

- Mike Tyson could be returning into the ring anytime soon

- The legendary boxer has been offered $1.1million to fight McNeeley

- Tyson defeated McNeeley in 1995 in a fight that lasted 89 seconds

Peter McNeeley who was beaten by Mike Tyson in a fight that lasted just 89 seconds in 1995 has called on the legend for a rematch after 25 years.

Mike beat the ‘Hurricane’ after just 89 seconds of the first round that night, but McNeeley is now ready for a re-match.

Peter McNeeley was floored twice in the first bout in 1995 before his corner threw in the towel just 89 seconds into the first round.
