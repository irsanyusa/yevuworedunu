James Blake (Tennis Player) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Profession: Tennis Player

Biography: James Blake is an American tennis player who won 10 singles finals, and appeared at 24 in his career. His highest ranking during this time was world No. 4. Blake also played a key role in the United States' 2007 Davis Cup victory.

Born: December 28, 1979

Age: 44 years old
