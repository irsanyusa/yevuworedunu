XOMG POP! Pays a Heartwarming Visit to Children Battling Cancer

Publish date: 2024-07-19

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment

Photo by Alberto E. Rodriguez/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

XOMG POP!, along with popular celebrities JoJo Siwa and Meghan Trainor, recently came together to give five children fighting cancer the best day ever. The group’s YouTube channel has just released a heartwarming video capturing the event.

XOMG POP! Helps Out Children Fighting Cancer

In the latest heartwarming news, XOMG POP!, the young girl group that loves singing, dancing, fashion, and girl power, recently teamed up with beloved celebrities Siwa and Trainor to give five children fighting cancer a day they will never forget.

The event was organized in support of Dancers Against Cancer, a non-profit organization that raises funds for cancer research and supports families affected by the disease. It took place at Studio Siwa, the dance studio owned by Siwa herself. The recently released video on XOMG POP’s YouTube channel shows how love, support, and friendship can truly make a difference in the world.

The video shows the children receiving makeovers, having fun at a fashion show, and dancing with their idols. With huge smiles on their faces, the little girls were able to forget about their struggles for a little while. Being able to just enjoy themselves all thanks to the kindness and generosity of XOMG POP!, Siwa, and Trainor.

XOMG POP!, which consists of Bella, Brooklynn, Kinley, Dallas, and Tinie T, have been friends since they were young and share a passion for music and dance. Their debut album, Party Like a Pop Star, features 12 original songs that are sure to get audiences singing.

The event was held in support of a great cause, Dancers Against Cancer, which was founded by dancer and choreographer Erin Benson. Benson founded it after she lost her friend and dance partner to cancer. The organization aims to create awareness, raise funds, and provide financial support to people affected by cancer and their families. Anyone who wants to donate to this worthy cause can visit their website.

XOMG POP! Are Releasing New Merchandise After Licensing Deals

In a joint statement, Jess and JoJo Siwa expressed their excitement about the upcoming merchandise. They are also excited about the partnership with Thomas Global Media. They revealed that XOMG POP! plans to release their books in the spring of 2024.

The extensive lineup of products includes accessories, apparel, arts and crafts sets, beauty and bath items, bedding and home goods. The products continue with costumes, computers, film and television projects, food and beverage offerings, games and puzzles, and travel accessories. Several product categories will hit retail stores this year, with more to follow in 2024.

With these licensing deals and the group’s ever-growing popularity, XOMG POP! continues to captivate audiences and expand its presence across various platforms and merchandise categories. The group’s positive messages have resonated with fans of all ages, making them an important cultural force.
