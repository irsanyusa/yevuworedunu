Beyonc, Jay Z, Blue Ivy Slayed in Barbie Halloween Costumes

Publish date: 2024-07-10

November 1, 2016 11:07 AM EDT

Although it didn’t seem like Beyoncé and Blue Ivy could top their epic Salt-N-Pepa get-up from Sunday, with the help of Jay Z, the pair may have just pulled it off. The famous family donned Barbie-themed outfits for Halloween, dressing as “Black Barbie,” “40th Anniversary Ken” and mini Barbie to celebrate the Halloween festivities.

Queen Bee took to Instagram Tuesday to share several pictures of the group modeling the costumes, showing off their plastic perfection in various doll-like poses. Bey and Jay even wore special collector’s edition boxes over their ensembles, completing the look.
