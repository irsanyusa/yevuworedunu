NPower recruitment process and requirements in 2024: all the info you need

Publish date: 2024-08-07

The federal government established the N-Power Program to improve the lives of middle-class people in Nigeria. It offers the youth tech skills training and quality job placements. The NPower initiative is part of the National Social Investment Management System (NASIMS) and the best place for acquiring innovative skills. If you are interested in the latest information about the NPower recruitment process and requirements, this article shares the essential details you should know.

NPower promotes economic diversification in Nigeria by teaching the youth globally competitive skills. Because of this, most NPower graduates develop progressive innovations for the international and domestic markets.

Basic requirements for NPower recruitment in 2024

The NPower administration has made its training programs available countrywide. Nonetheless, applicants should meet the set requirements to be enrolled. These are the requirements for NPower recruitment or enrollment:

  • You must be a Nigerian citizen by birth.
  • You should be at least 18 years old.
  • You must not be a beneficiary of any NPower Program.

How do I apply for NPower recruitment?

You can only apply on NPower's official website. After sending your application, you can log into the portal and do an online exam. After that, check the portal regularly to know your selection status.

How to apply for NPower recruitment in 2024?

NPower programs require committed and dedicated students. Besides, each student is assigned a mentor to guide them through their training program. If you are sure that this is what you want, below is the application process:

  • Visit NPower's official website.
  • Tap "Click to Apply."
  • Enter your email address and click "continue."
  • The system will display a Bio-data form.
  • Enter your name, gender, Nigerian State, LGA, phone number, home address, and date of birth in the Bio-data form.
  • Click "continue" when done and follow the prompts until you complete the process.

The NPower 2023/2024 recruitment process is ongoing. All Nigerian citizens who meet the recruitment requirements are invited to apply.

You will only be accepted into the program of your choice if you pass the NPower exams on the recruitment portal. Also, you can only be able to set your NPower recruitment portal login password after applying.

Applicants who pass their online exams on NPower's recruitment portal get their Deployment Letters. If you do not complete your application or NPower does not approve your application, you will not be able to take the test and will not receive a Deployment Letter.

How to register for NPower recruitment in 2024?

You can only register for NPower recruitment on NPower's official website.

Can I change my details on the NPower recruitment portal?

You can always change your NPower ID, BVN, NIN, and bank account numbers on the dashboard of the NPower recruitment portal. Candidates not pre-selected can re-validate their records to be pre-selected.

NPower contact details

Since NPower is part of the National Social Investment Management System (NASIMS), you can contact the NPower's support center through the NASIMS contact channels for help regarding the NPower recruitment process, requirements, and challenges you are facing on the portal:

  • Call: 092203102, 018888148
  • Email: support.npower@nasims.gov.ng
  • Live chat: www.nasims.gov.ng
