7 celebrities who relocated from Nigeria after hitting fame (IGTGV)

Publish date: 2024-09-05

7 celebrities who relocated from Nigeria after hitting fame These guys came, saw fame, made the headlines, and left while the ovation was still loud. 1. Victoria Inyama: Popular in the early 1990s and 2000s for her 'bad girl' roles in movies, Victoria Inyama left Nollywood after she got married to Godwin Okri. Though their marriage has hit the rocks, Inyama still lives in the UK with her three kids. 2. Pat Attah: One of the biggest names in Nollywood in the 1990s and early 2000s. Reports say he became a born again Christian and relocated to Germany with his family. 3. Dolly Unachukwu: For many who grew up watching Nollywood movies in the 1990s, Dolly Unachukwu’s name might still ring a bell. The actress relocated to the United Kingdom in 2000 after she remarried for the second time. She still lives in the UK with her daughter. 4. Karen Igho: She shot to fame after her stint on Big Brother Africa in 2011 where she became the first woman to win the reality TV show. She got married to Yaroslav Rakos in 2014 and they have kids together. She currently resides in the United States of America. 5. Regina Askia: She is unarguably one of the biggest actors of her time. After several years in entertainment, Regina relocated to the United States of America where she practices as a Nurse. She got married to an American, Rudolph 'Rudy' Williams and they have three kids together. 6. Sandra Achums: The talented actress left Nigeria in 2006 after she got married to her husband, Tony. The couple is based in Germany with their kids 7. Georgina Onuoha: Georgina relocated to the United States of America after she tied the knot with her ex-husband, Dr C. Igwegbe.
